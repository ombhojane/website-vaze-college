V G Vaze College of Arts Science and Commerce, Mumbai

Rating 4.3/5 (Student Reviews)

College Infrastructure: 4.1/5
Academics: 4.1/5
Placements: 3.4/5
Value for Money: 4.1/5
Campus Life: 4.3/5

College Infrastructure: The college is pretty huge and it basically has everything that an ideal college should have in it's campus. There's a lot of greenery and overall it gives out a good vibe and urges us to actually come to college.

Academics: Maybe because of the pandemic and online learning, education is not that fun. But BMM lectures that I attend are really interactive and it helps build a bond with the faculties. It is a very safe and open space wherein students can express themselves.

Placements: I am totally new to this college so I don't really have a proper idea as to how the whole placement thing works. But a lot of big celebrities have been produced by the college so I feel that the placements might be apt.

Value-for-money: The fees for my BMM course is about 19 thousand per year which is pretty feasible and it is adequate.
